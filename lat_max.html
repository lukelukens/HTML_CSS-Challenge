Max Temperature

As latitude increases, observed temperatures decrease. Although one may expect peak temperatures to cluster around zero degrees latitude, the tilt of the Earth causes a plateau effect between -20 and +20 that mirrors the Earth's curavture. This tilt is relevant in the cloud cover visualization as well.
